1. Overview

Welcome to Stellar Vortex, a sci-fi space shooter mobile game built with Flutter and Flame. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy details the information practices for the Stellar Vortex game, available on the Google Play Store and iOS App Store.

Because we value your trust, the game has been designed from the ground up to operate offline, locally, and securely, without requiring unnecessary device access or collecting user profile data.

🔍

2. Information Collection & Use

Stellar Vortex is built with user privacy as a priority:

  • No Personal Data: We do not collect, request, or store any personal data such as your name, email address, phone number, location, or contacts.
  • No Tracking: The game does not feature any telemetry, analytics engines, SDK tracking libraries, or diagnostic reporting tools that send profile details over the network.
  • No Ads: The game is completely ad-free and contains no third-party advertising SDKs that gather behavioral data.
  • No Social Integration: There are no social media login options or external profile sync modules.
💾

3. Local Storage & Permissions

To provide a full-featured gaming experience, Stellar Vortex stores certain settings locally:

  • High Scores & Progression: Your highest achieved scores and unlocked achievements are saved directly to your device.
  • Preferences: Game settings (such as sound volume, music controls, and selected language) are kept locally.
  • Device Location: This data never leaves your device and is not shared with any external servers. If you delete or reinstall the game, this local data will be reset.
👶

4. Children's Privacy

Our game is designed to be safe, entertaining, and accessible to players of all ages, including children.

Since Stellar Vortex does not collect any personal information or device IDs, it is fully compliant with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) standards for minors. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

🔄

5. Policy Changes

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be posted directly on this webpage. We recommend reviewing this page periodically to stay informed of our commitment to user privacy.
